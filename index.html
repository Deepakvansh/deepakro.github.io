Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RO water purifiers

How often should I replace RO filters?

Pre-filters should be replaced every 6-9 months, carbon filters every 9-12 months, and the RO membrane every 2-3 years depending on your water quality and usage.

Is RO water safe for drinking?

Yes, RO water is completely safe for drinking. Our systems include mineralization stages to add back essential minerals removed during the purification process.

How much water is wasted in RO purification?

Traditional RO systems waste about 3-4 liters for every liter purified. Our advanced systems with zero wastage technology reduce this to less than 1 liter wasted per liter purified.

What maintenance does an RO system require?

Regular maintenance includes filter replacements as recommended, sanitizing the storage tank every 6 months, and checking system pressure annually. We offer comprehensive maintenance plans.
